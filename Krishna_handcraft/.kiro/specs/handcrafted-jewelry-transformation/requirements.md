# Requirements Document

## Introduction

Transform the existing minimalist shop template into a sophisticated handcrafted jewelry e-commerce platform. The transformation will maintain the clean, minimalist aesthetic while adapting content, imagery, and functionality specifically for artisan jewelry products.

## Glossary

- **Handcrafted_Jewelry**: Jewelry pieces made by hand using beads, threads, wires, and craft materials
- **Beadwork**: Jewelry created using various types of beads (glass, wood, stone, ceramic, etc.)
- **Collection**: A curated group of jewelry pieces with a common theme, color scheme, or technique
- **Product_Gallery**: Multi-angle product photography showcasing beadwork details and craftsmanship
- **Customization_Options**: Available modifications like color variations, length adjustments, or bead substitutions
- **Care_Instructions**: Specific guidance for maintaining beaded and craft jewelry
- **Craft_Materials**: Beads, threads, wires, findings, and other materials used in handmade jewelry
- **Jewelry_Techniques**: Methods like beading, wire wrapping, macramé, embroidery, etc.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Brand Identity Transformation

**User Story:** As a handcrafted jewelry store owner, I want to rebrand the site for beaded and craft jewelry, so that customers immediately understand we specialize in handmade beadwork and artisan craft jewelry.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user visits the homepage, THE System SHALL display craft jewelry-focused branding with an appropriate logo and tagline emphasizing handmade beadwork
2. WHEN displaying the hero section, THE System SHALL showcase handcrafted beaded jewelry imagery with messaging about artisan craftsmanship and creativity
3. WHEN showing navigation links, THE System SHALL use craft jewelry-specific categories (Beaded Necklaces, Bracelets, Earrings, Wire Jewelry, Custom Pieces)
4. THE System SHALL update all placeholder text to reflect beadwork terminology and handcraft values
5. WHEN displaying the sustainability section, THE System SHALL focus on eco-friendly materials and sustainable craft practices

### Requirement 2: Product Content Adaptation

**User Story:** As a customer, I want to see craft jewelry-specific product information, so that I can make informed purchasing decisions about handmade beaded pieces.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing product listings, THE System SHALL display beaded jewelry items with appropriate names, descriptions, and pricing
2. WHEN showing product details, THE System SHALL include craft jewelry-specific information (bead types, thread materials, techniques used, dimensions)
3. WHEN displaying product images, THE System SHALL show multiple angles and close-up shots of beadwork details
4. THE System SHALL replace generic product categories with craft jewelry categories (Beadwork, Wire Jewelry, Macramé, Statement Pieces, etc.)
5. WHEN showing product specifications, THE System SHALL include bead materials, thread types, closure information, and care instructions

### Requirement 3: Visual Content Replacement

**User Story:** As a potential customer, I want to see beautiful beaded jewelry photography, so that I can appreciate the craftsmanship and intricate details of the handmade pieces.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN loading any page, THE System SHALL display high-quality beaded jewelry photography instead of generic product images
2. WHEN viewing the hero section, THE System SHALL show lifestyle imagery of people wearing handcrafted beaded jewelry
3. WHEN browsing product grids, THE System SHALL display beaded jewelry pieces with clear detail shots showing bead patterns and textures
4. THE System SHALL ensure all craft jewelry images highlight the handmade nature and unique characteristics
5. WHEN showing detail views, THE System SHALL include macro photography highlighting beadwork patterns and craftsmanship details

### Requirement 4: Craft Jewelry-Specific Features

**User Story:** As a beaded jewelry shopper, I want craft jewelry-specific functionality, so that I can find and customize pieces that suit my style and preferences.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN browsing products, THE System SHALL provide filters for bead type, color scheme, technique, price range, and jewelry type
2. WHEN viewing product details, THE System SHALL display length options and adjustment possibilities for necklaces and bracelets
3. WHEN interested in customization, THE System SHALL show available options (color variations, bead substitutions, length adjustments)
4. THE System SHALL include care instructions specific to each piece's materials (beads, threads, findings)
5. WHEN searching, THE System SHALL support craft jewelry-specific search terms (beaded, handmade, wire-wrapped, macramé, etc.)

### Requirement 5: Artisan Story Integration

**User Story:** As a customer interested in handcrafted goods, I want to learn about the artisans and their beadwork techniques, so that I can connect with the story and creativity behind each piece.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing the about section, THE System SHALL tell the story of the craft jewelry artisans and their beadwork techniques
2. WHEN browsing collections, THE System SHALL include information about the inspiration, color choices, and creation process
3. THE System SHALL highlight the handmade nature and uniqueness of each beaded piece
4. WHEN viewing product details, THE System SHALL include information about the techniques used and time invested in creation
5. THE System SHALL emphasize creativity, personal expression, and the artistic value of handcrafted beadwork

### Requirement 6: Enhanced Product Photography Display

**User Story:** As a beaded jewelry customer, I want to see detailed product photography, so that I can examine the bead patterns, colors, and craftsmanship before purchasing.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing product details, THE System SHALL provide zoom functionality for examining bead details and patterns
2. WHEN browsing product images, THE System SHALL show beaded jewelry from multiple angles (front, back, side, worn, detail shots)
3. THE System SHALL include lifestyle shots showing beaded jewelry being worn in natural settings
4. WHEN viewing collections, THE System SHALL display coordinated pieces and color-matching suggestions
5. THE System SHALL ensure photography highlights the texture, colors, and intricate patterns of beadwork

### Requirement 7: Craft Jewelry Care and Information

**User Story:** As a beaded jewelry owner, I want detailed care instructions and product information, so that I can maintain my handcrafted pieces properly.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN purchasing beaded jewelry, THE System SHALL provide specific care instructions for different bead materials and threads
2. WHEN viewing product details, THE System SHALL include detailed material specifications (bead types, thread materials, findings)
3. THE System SHALL provide guidance on proper storage to prevent tangling and damage
4. WHEN displaying warranties, THE System SHALL include information about repair services and bead replacement
5. THE System SHALL offer guidance on cleaning different types of beads and maintaining thread integrity

### Requirement 8: Collection and Category Organization

**User Story:** As a craft jewelry shopper, I want to browse by technique and style, so that I can easily find pieces that match my aesthetic preferences.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN navigating the site, THE System SHALL organize products by craft technique (Beadwork, Wire Wrapping, Macramé, Embroidered Jewelry)
2. WHEN browsing collections, THE System SHALL group pieces by color theme, style, or occasion
3. THE System SHALL provide filtering options for bead material, color palette, and technique
4. WHEN viewing categories, THE System SHALL display appropriate technique descriptions and styling inspiration
5. THE System SHALL suggest complementary pieces and coordinated color schemes
