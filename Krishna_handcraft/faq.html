local_shipping Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take? expand_more

We ship within 2-3 business days of receiving your order. Delivery typically takes:

  • Domestic (India): 5-7 business days
  • Metro cities: 3-5 business days
  • International: 10-15 business days

You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships!

Do you offer free shipping? expand_more
Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹2,000 within India. For international orders, free shipping is available on orders above $100.
Can I track my order? expand_more
Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and link to track your package in real-time.

inventory_2 Products & Materials

What materials do you use? expand_more

We use high-quality, sustainable materials including:

  • Natural stone beads (turquoise, jade, agate)
  • Glass beads in various colors and finishes
  • Wooden beads from sustainable sources
  • Silver-plated and copper wire
  • Organic cotton cord for macramé
  • Hypoallergenic findings and clasps
Are your products handmade? expand_more
Yes! Every piece is lovingly handcrafted by our founder Rashika. Each item is unique and made with care, attention to detail, and traditional artisan techniques.
Can I request custom jewelry? expand_more
Absolutely! We love creating custom pieces. You can choose bead colors, materials, lengths, and designs. Contact us at thevrindacreation@gmail.com with your ideas, and we'll work with you to create your perfect piece!

favorite Care & Maintenance

How do I care for my beaded jewelry? expand_more

To keep your jewelry beautiful:

  • Store flat in a dry place to prevent tangling
  • Clean gently with a soft, dry cloth
  • Avoid water exposure, especially for wooden beads
  • Keep away from perfumes and lotions
  • Remove before swimming or bathing
What if my jewelry breaks? expand_more
We offer free repairs for the first year on all our jewelry! Just contact us and we'll arrange for repair or replacement. After the first year, we offer repair services at a minimal cost.

Yes! We're happy to exchange items within 15 days of purchase. Exchanges are subject to availability. Contact us to arrange an exchange.
